Max Bennett is the author of *A Brief History of Intelligence* (2023), a book tracing brain evolution through five major breakthroughs. His background spans neuroscience and AI. The book argues for evolutionary decomposition as methodology: understanding the human brain by tracing the sequence of innovations from simple nervous systems to complex ones.
