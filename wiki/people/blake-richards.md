Computational neuroscientist at McGill University and Mila; senior author of [[guerguiev-2017-segregated-dendrites|Guerguiev et al. (2017)]] and co-author on [[payeur-2020-burst-dependent-credit-assignment|Payeur et al. (2020)]]. Richards' group focuses on how dendritic computation enables biologically plausible credit assignment in cortical hierarchies. The Guerguiev → Payeur progression from his lab represents the main spiking-neuron thread in the credit assignment lineage, complementing the rate-based thread from the Senn lab ([[sacramento-2018-dendritic-microcircuits-backprop|Sacramento et al., 2018]]).
