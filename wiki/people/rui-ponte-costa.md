Computational neuroscientist at University of Bristol; bridges the Senn lab (Bern) and Richards lab threads of the credit assignment lineage. Co-author of [[sacramento-2018-dendritic-microcircuits-backprop|Sacramento et al. (2018)]] (from the Senn group) and senior author of [[greedy-2022-single-phase-burstccn|Greedy et al. (2022)]] (BurstCCN). Also co-authored Costa et al. (2017) on cortical microcircuits as gated-recurrent neural networks. Research focuses on biologically plausible learning algorithms and cortical circuit models.
