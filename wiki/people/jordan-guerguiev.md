Computational neuroscientist; first author of [[guerguiev-2017-segregated-dendrites|Guerguiev et al. (2017)]], the first spiking implementation of deep learning with segregated dendritic compartments. Also co-author on [[payeur-2020-burst-dependent-credit-assignment|Payeur et al. (2020)]], which extended this work with burst-dependent plasticity and continuous (phaseless) operation. PhD work at University of Toronto with Blake Richards.
