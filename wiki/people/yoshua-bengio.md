Pioneer of deep learning; Turing Award recipient (2018, with Hinton and LeCun). Relevant to the wiki through his work on biologically plausible alternatives to backpropagation — target propagation (Bengio, 2014; Lee et al., 2015), equilibrium propagation (Scellier & Bengio, 2017), and the dendritic microcircuit model of [[sacramento-2018-dendritic-microcircuits-backprop|Sacramento et al. (2018)]]. His involvement signals the convergence of the deep learning and computational neuroscience communities on the [[Credit Assignment]] problem.
