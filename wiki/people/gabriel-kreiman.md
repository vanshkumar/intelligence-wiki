Gabriel Kreiman is a neuroscientist at Harvard Medical School and Boston Children's Hospital. His lab works at the intersection of computational neuroscience and biological vision, with interests in neural prediction and emergent behavior.

## Relevant Work

- Senior author on [[li-2024-prediction-noise-reward|Li et al. (2024)]], demonstrating that local prediction + noise produces emergent reward-seeking behavior in neural networks (the PaN algorithm).
