Neuroscientist studying how prefrontal cortex represents learned categories, using chronic two-photon calcium imaging in behaving mice to track neural representations across the entire course of learning.

## Key Contributions in This Wiki

- Senior/corresponding author on study showing gradual emergence of category-selective neurons in mouse mPFC during visual categorization learning, with flexible remapping during rule switches ([[reinert-2021-pfc-categorization|Reinert et al., 2021]])
