Computational neuroscientist who has worked on cortical dynamics, visual processing, and biologically grounded learning rules. At ETH/UNI Zurich, co-developed the two-site synaptic integration framework with [[Konrad Kording]], proposing that the basal/apical dendritic compartments of pyramidal neurons provide the architectural substrate needed for gradient-based learning — a conceptual precursor to the burst-dependent credit assignment lineage ([[payeur-2020-burst-dependent-credit-assignment|Payeur et al. 2020]]).

## Key Contributions in This Wiki

- Co-proposed the two-site integration framework: apical dendrites carry the learning signal (burst rate), basal dendrites carry the activity signal (firing rate), enabling backpropagation and self-supervised learning in biologically plausible networks ([[kording-konig-2001-two-integration-sites|Kording & Konig, 2001]])
