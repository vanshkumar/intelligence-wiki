Associate Professor at MIT McGovern Institute. Lab focuses on dendritic biophysics and computation in cortical circuits — how the structure and biophysical properties of dendrites shape neural computation. Senior and corresponding author of [[francioni-2026-vectorized-dendritic-signals|Francioni et al. (2026)]], which provides the first in vivo experimental evidence that distal apical dendrites carry cell-specific instructive signals during learning. The Harnett lab's expertise in simultaneous soma-dendrite two-photon imaging was critical to this demonstration.
