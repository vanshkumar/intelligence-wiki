Computational neuroscientist; co-first author (with Heng Wei Zhu) of [[greedy-2022-single-phase-burstccn|Greedy et al. (2022)]], which introduced BurstCCN — the synthesis model that resolves the phase and depth-scaling limitations of the dendritic credit assignment lineage. PhD student in Rui Ponte Costa's group at the Bristol Computational Neuroscience Unit.
