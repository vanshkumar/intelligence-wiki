Paul Sternberg is a geneticist and developmental biologist at Caltech whose lab has been central to *C. elegans* research for decades, with work spanning cell fate specification, signaling, behavior, and neurogenetics. The lab co-developed WormBase and has contributed extensively to the genetic dissection of *C. elegans* sensory and motor circuits.
