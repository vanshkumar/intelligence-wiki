Athanassios (Thanos) Siapas is a neuroscientist at Caltech in the Division of Biology and Division of Engineering and Applied Science. His lab studies hippocampal circuits, oscillatory dynamics, and sleep.

## Relevant Work

- Senior author on [[lubenov-2009-theta-travelling-waves|Lubenov & Siapas (2009)]], which demonstrated that hippocampal theta oscillations are travelling waves, overturning the prevailing view of synchronized theta.
- Senior author on [[wierzynski-2009-sleep-hpc-pfc|Wierzynski et al. (2009)]], which demonstrated directional spike timing from hippocampal CA1 to mPFC during slow-wave sleep, driven by sharp-wave/ripple events — providing single-cell-pair evidence for hippocampus-to-cortex memory transfer during sleep.

Together these papers span the Siapas lab's work on hippocampal circuit dynamics across behavioral states: theta travelling waves during waking spatial behavior, SWR-driven hippocampal-cortical coupling during SWS.
