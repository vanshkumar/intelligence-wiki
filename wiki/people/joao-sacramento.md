Computational neuroscientist working on biologically plausible learning algorithms. First author of [[sacramento-2018-dendritic-microcircuits-backprop|Sacramento et al. (2018)]], which showed that dendritic cortical microcircuits with three-compartment pyramidal neurons and lateral SST interneurons approximate backpropagation via local dendritic prediction errors — unifying [[Predictive Coding]] with [[Credit Assignment]]. Previously at University of Bern (Walter Senn's group); later at ETH Zurich.
