Computational neuroscientist at the University of Bern; senior author of [[sacramento-2018-dendritic-microcircuits-backprop|Sacramento et al. (2018)]]. Senn's group developed the **dendritic predictive plasticity** framework (Urbanczik & Senn, 2014) — the family of local learning rules dw/dt = η[φ(u) − φ(v)]r in which plasticity is driven by mismatches between compartmental potentials. This framework underpins both Sacramento et al. (2018) and is closely related to the burst-dependent credit assignment lineage ([[payeur-2020-burst-dependent-credit-assignment|Payeur et al., 2020]]). CIFAR Senior Fellow.
