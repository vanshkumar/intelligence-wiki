Alexander Proekt is a neuroscientist at the University of Pennsylvania (Department of Anesthesiology and Critical Care). His work focuses on extracting principles of neural dynamics from experimental recordings, with emphasis on macroscopic dynamical models that generalize across individuals.

## Relevant Work

- [[brennan-2019-conserved-macroscopic-dynamics|Brennan & Proekt (2019)]] — conserved macroscopic dynamics in *C. elegans*; asymmetric diffusion map modeling
- [[brennan-2023-looper-computational-scaffold|Brennan et al. (2023)]] — LOOPER method for extracting computational scaffolds from neural dynamics; cross-system comparison (monkey PFC vs. RNN)
