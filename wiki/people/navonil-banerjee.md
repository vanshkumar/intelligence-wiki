Navonil Banerjee is a neuroscientist working in Elissa Hallem's lab at UCLA on sensory circuit function in *C. elegans*, with emphasis on how life-stage and physiological state reconfigure chemosensory processing.

## Relevant Work in This Wiki

- [[banerjee-2023-life-stage-chemosensation|Banerjee et al. (2023)]] (first author) — differential processing of CO₂ across life stages sharing the same behavioral valence in *C. elegans*.
