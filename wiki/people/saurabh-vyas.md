Saurabh Vyas is a systems neuroscientist whose work with the Shenoy lab established that motor adaptation operates on preparatory dimensions — learning repositions the initial condition of the movement dynamics rather than retuning movement-period activity. First author of the 2020 *Annual Review of Neuroscience* that codifies the [[Computation Through Dynamics|CTD]] framework.

## Relevant Work in This Wiki

- [[vyas-2020-computation-through-dynamics|Vyas, Golub, Sussillo & Shenoy (2020)]] (first author) — review codifying CTD; also author of the underlying VMR-adaptation-via-preparation results (Vyas 2018, 2020).
