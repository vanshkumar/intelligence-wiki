Neuroscientist studying the computational capabilities of individual biological neurons, particularly dendritic computation. Uses machine learning benchmarks to quantify what single neurons can do.

## Key Contributions in This Wiki

- Demonstrated that a single neuron modeled with dendritic branching nonlinearities (the *k*-tree) can solve complex image classification tasks, approaching the performance of multi-neuron networks ([[jones-2020-dendritic-computation-power|Jones & Kording, 2020]])
